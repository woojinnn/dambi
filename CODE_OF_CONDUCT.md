# Code of Conduct

Dambi contributors and maintainers should keep project discussions technical,
respectful, and focused on improving wallet-signing safety.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful of different experience levels and backgrounds.
- Discuss code, evidence, and tradeoffs directly.
- Keep security-sensitive details private until maintainers coordinate
  disclosure.
- Assume good intent, but correct unsafe assumptions clearly.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private vulnerability details before coordinated disclosure.
- Sharing secrets, live exploit payloads, or user data in public issues.
- Repeated off-topic disruption of issues, pull requests, or discussions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove comments that violate these
expectations. Repeated or severe violations may lead to blocked participation in
the repository.

Security reports should follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
